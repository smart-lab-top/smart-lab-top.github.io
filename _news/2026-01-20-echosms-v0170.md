We are excited to announce the release of [**EchoSMs v0.17.0**](https://github.com/ices-tools-dev/echoSMs/releases/tag/v0.17.0)! 

This update introduces a significant new feature: the **Shape Viewer**, which enhances the visualization capabilities of the tool. This feature was contributed and refined by our team member (@jfshanghai).

Key updates in this release:
- **Shape Viewer**: A new tool for visualizing acoustic shapes within EchoSMs.
- Various enhancements and stability improvements.

For more details, check out the full release notes on GitHub: [https://github.com/ices-tools-dev/echoSMs/releases/tag/v0.17.0](https://github.com/ices-tools-dev/echoSMs/releases/tag/v0.17.0)
