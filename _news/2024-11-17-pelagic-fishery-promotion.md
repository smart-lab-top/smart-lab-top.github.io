### Featured Technology

Our team presented an innovative system designed to enhance the efficiency and sustainability of pelagic fishing operations:

1.  **Shipborne Fish Finder Data Processing Terminal**: A high-performance system for real-time analysis of acoustic data from commercial echosounders.

During the conference, our researchers provided live demonstrations to industry experts and enterprise representatives. The positive feedback received highlights the practical value of our research in the global pelagic fishery sector. We look forward to further industry collaborations to promote these technologies.
