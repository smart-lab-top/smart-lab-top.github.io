Opportunities to join the SMART Lab team

Join the SMART Lab Team

Are you passionate about marine science, acoustics, and cutting-edge research? Join our dynamic team at the SMART Lab!

Current Opportunities

📚 Master Students

We welcome motivated master’s students interested in:

  • Fisheries acoustics and marine bioacoustics
  • Underwater signal processing
  • Environmental monitoring
  • Data analysis and modeling

Requirements:

  • Background in engineering, physics, biology, or related fields
  • Strong interest in marine science and acoustics
  • Good programming skills (Python, MATLAB preferred)

🎓 PhD Students

We offer PhD positions for exceptional candidates with:

  • Research experience in acoustics, signal processing, or marine biology
  • Publication record in peer-reviewed journals
  • Independent research capabilities
  • Interest in interdisciplinary work

🔬 Research Assistants

Opportunities for undergraduate or graduate students to gain hands-on research experience in:

  • Field data collection
  • Laboratory analysis
  • Data processing and visualization
  • Scientific writing

🤖 AI/ML Research

Specialized positions for students interested in applying AI/ML to marine acoustics:

  • Machine learning for acoustic signal classification
  • Computer vision for underwater imagery
  • Big data analysis for marine ecosystems

How to Apply

Step 1: Review Our Research

  • Visit our publications page
  • Check our projects page
  • Learn about our current research directions

Step 2: Contact Us

Send your CV and a brief cover letter to our lab director:

Dr. Jianfeng Tong
Email: jftong@shou.edu.cn
Subject: “[Your Name] - Interest in Joining SMART Lab”

Include:

  • Your academic background and GPA
  • Research interests and experience
  • Why you’re interested in joining our lab
  • Any relevant skills or projects

Step 3: Interview Process

  • Initial email exchange
  • Virtual or in-person interview
  • Research project discussion
  • Final decision

What We Offer

🎯 Research Excellence

  • Cutting-edge research facilities
  • International collaboration opportunities
  • Access to advanced acoustic equipment
  • Publication support in top journals

🌟 Professional Development

  • Regular seminars and workshops
  • Conference travel support
  • Skill development training
  • Career mentoring

🌊 Unique Environment

  • Beautiful campus at Shanghai Ocean University
  • Access to marine research vessels
  • Collaboration with leading oceanographic institutions
  • Vibrant research community

Current Team Composition

Our lab currently includes:

  • 1 Principal Investigator
  • 2 PhD Students
  • 2 Master Students
  • Research Assistants (seasonal)

Application Timeline

  • Master Positions: Applications accepted year-round, with priority given to applications received by March 1 for fall admission
  • PhD Positions: Rolling admissions, but we recommend applying 6-12 months before desired start date
  • Research Assistantships: Available throughout the academic year

International Students

We welcome applications from international students. Shanghai Ocean University provides support for international applicants including:

  • Chinese language courses
  • Visa assistance
  • Housing support
  • Cultural integration programs

We value diversity and encourage applications from students of all backgrounds. The SMART Lab is committed to creating an inclusive and supportive research environment.

If you have questions about any of our opportunities, don’t hesitate to contact us.
